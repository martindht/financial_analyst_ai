Calculator-––%-––%-––%-––%-––%-––%-––%Apple's Teen Dominance Is Intact, Analyst Sees Potential for Record iPhone Ownershipby Anusuya Lahiri, Benzinga Editor October 11, 2023 3:12 PM | 1 min read Piper Sandler analyst Harish V. Kumar had an Overweight rating on Apple Inc AAPL with a price target of $220.Apple's share of smartphone ownership remains near record highs in Piper Sandler's Taking Stock with Teens Fall 2023 survey (note). Of the ~9,200 respondents, 87% have an iPhone, slightly below the 88% record set in the Spring 2021 survey. In addition, the iPhone could return to record highs due to the 88% purchase intention among teens. Kumar notes over 23% of teens plan to upgrade to an iPhone 15 this Fall/Winter, slightly below the 25% purchase intention for the iPhone 14 last spring. Apple's other hardware metrics also showed strong results, with more than 34% of teens already owning an Apple Watch and 72% owning AirPods near record highs.  The analyst views the survey results as a sign that Apple's place as the dominant device brand among teens remains well intact.The analyst projects Q4 revenue of $88.89 billion vs. consensus $89.22 billion and EPS of $1.37 vs. consensus $1.39. Kumar expects FY23 revenue of $382.67 billion vs. consensus of $383.11 billion and EPS of $6.04 vs. consensus of $6.07.Price Action: AAPL shares traded higher by 0.11% at $178.62 on the last check Wednesday.EarningsAnalyst RatingsOptionsDividendsIPOsClick to see more Analyst Ratings updates© 2023 Benzinga.com.
